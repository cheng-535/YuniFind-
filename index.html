Meet Up -

Safe & Convenient

With “Meet Up,” you can trade directly on campus skip delivery fees and waiting times. Simply agree on a safe, familiar spot like the canteen, hostel lobby, or faculty entrance. You’ll deal only with fellow students, ensuring safety, trust, and a more personal, efficient transaction.

Only Campus -

Students Only

This platform is for university students only. All users must verify with a university email or student ID, ensuring a safe, relevant marketplace filled with student-focused items like textbooks, electronics, and essentials no outsiders, no spam.

Make Offer -

Bargain Like a Pro

Not happy with the price? Use “Make Offer” to send your own price directly to the seller. They can accept, reject, or counter in chat no awkward in-person bargaining, just quick, fair deals both sides will appreciate.

Easy To Transfer -

Easy Trade

We know students want simple, fast deals. That’s why we support easy hand-to-hand cash payments and provide safety guides for smooth transactions. If both sides agree, you can also use optional online payment. From used laptops to second-hand phone cases, every deal stays clear, safe, and hassle-free.

Get instant answers to your most common questions without waiting. Our smart support system is designed to save you time by offering clear, step-by-step solutions whenever you need them. No long queues, no delays just reliable help, right when you need it.
